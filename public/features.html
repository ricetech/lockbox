WHAT LOCKBOX HAS TO OFFER

Have you ever had to block out a whole day to wait for a single package? Whether it’s coming home early from work or cancelling plans for a delivery in fear of it getting stolen and/or having to travel distances to the post office. This is a very big inconvenience you shouldn't be worrying about. Lockbox is a delivering-service that keeps your packages safe by allowing users to customize their delivery time and make sure they are home for their package.

CONVENIENT

Lockbox stores your packages until you’re ready for it.

TIME SAVING

  • You tell us when you want your package, and it shows up at your door.
  • One hour timeframe, which is much more reasonable than the 12-14 hours.
  • Lockbox stores your packages until you’re ready for it.

AFFORDABLE

  • You only pay for what you use - no subscription fee.
  • Discounts if you take an off-peak delivery slot.
  • An option to restrict social media apps and/ or games during work hours, allow users to be more efficient in their work life by eliminating distractions.

RELIABLE

  • There will be a compensation if we miss the window.
